WORLD'S BEST FACILITIES

The Sports School strives to provide our students with one of the best state-of-the-art sporting and academic infrastructures in India to prepare them for the highly competitive world of sports.

Our sports facilities feature a remarkably functional design, comfortable user facilities, and the ability to expand per needs. In addition to that, the infrastructure is equipped with all the requirements to host national and international level sporting events.

The Sports School is equipped with new-age facilities that aid the students to elevate their everyday life and give a comfortable space to pursue their passion. Academic facilities include unbeatable installationsthat are designed to optimize the intellectual, personal, and social development of young sportspeople.

SPORTS

    Tennis

  • 1 Centre Court | 6 Synthetic Courts | 2 Practice Clay Courts
  • 7 ITF Classified Acrylic Cushioned Tennis Courts with floodlights
  • The stadium court with seating/viewing capacity for about 200 people

    Badminton

  • Planned BWF standard 12 wooden indoor synthetic Badminton courts

    Football

  • FIFA Quality Pro Certified Artificial Turf measuring 108m x 68m Football Field

    Cricket

  • International standard 65m natural turf Cricket grounds, fit to host any International and National level Cricket matches
  • 6 outdoor practice nets

    Basketball

  • 2 FIBA standard Basketball courts and a half-court with floodlight facility

ACADEMICS

    Smart Classrooms

  • All the classrooms at The Sports School are extremely spacious and are equipped with ergonomically designed furniture. We also have classrooms with smart teaching resources so that the students can approach the study material more interactively. The activities in the classrooms happen in the areas defined by the students themselves.

    Labs and Library

  • The highly equipped library of The Sports School contains books, CD- ROMS, DVDs that cover a panoramic spectrum of subjects to satisfy the urge to learn and curiosity of our students while maintaining a supportive and nurturing environment in building their knowledge bank. Our labs such as science and maths are equipped with all the necessary equipment for students to practice.

    Real-time Learning

  • The Sports School’s commitment to preparing our students for the real world is profound. We encourage them to take part in activities like debates, extempore, and learning by doing. Our curriculum emphasizes learning, creativity, innovation, and collaboration to develop a deep understanding in one area of concentration.

OTHER FACILITIES

    Gymnasium

  • Gym Facilities at The Sports School include exercise rooms, free weight & other strength training equipment and a wide variety of cardio exercise equipment. It also features other facilities for overall wellness and provides essential opportunities to exercise.

    Video Analysis Room

  • In line with our mission of providing our students with the best possible infrastructure, the video analysis room at The Sports School allows students to take a peek into nuances of the game and understanding how they can improve their skills.

    Hostel

  • One of The Sports School’s defining characteristics is our residential community for our students. It has been designed to create a close-knit environment for living, studying, and relaxing. It is equipped with all modern amenities that will help a student feel right at home and provide them with a comfortable space to unwind after a power packed day.

    Cafeteria

  • We place a lot of emphasis on healthy eating and provide a balanced and nutritious diet at our school. Hygienically prepared veg and non-veg food is prepared to ensure it helps students with increased focus, strength, attention and better on and off-field well-being.

    Medications

  • The medical room at The Sports School is well equipped with primary health facilities and is looked after by a professional paramedic staff. We have a 24×7 in-house nurse to attend to any medical emergencies. It contains all the vital safety features and amenities that help to attend to each patient in a specialised manner.

    Auditorium

  • The Sports School’s auditorium features all the facilities necessary for hosting both national and international level events. It also allows seating of large crowds without any hassle of accommodation.
